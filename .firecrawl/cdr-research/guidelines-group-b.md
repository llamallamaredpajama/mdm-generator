- **ACOG Clinical Practice Updates Index** (ongoing 2025) — Includes topics such as concurrent sexual partner therapy, and multiple focused updates — https://www.acog.org/clinical/clinical-guidance/clinical-practice-update

- **Guidelines for Postoperative Care in Cesarean Delivery (ERAS)** (2025) — Enhanced Recovery After Surgery Society recommendations, Part 3, 2025 update — https://www.ajog.org/article/S0002-9378(25)00071-7/fulltext
