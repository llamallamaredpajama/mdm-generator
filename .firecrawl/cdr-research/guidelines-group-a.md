## Notes on Data Quality

- Guidelines marked as "living" (AGA, IDSA COVID-19) are continuously updated and may have interim changes not captured here.
- SAEM GRACE series numbering: GRACE-1 (chest pain), GRACE-2 (abdominal pain), GRACE-4 (alcohol withdrawal) — GRACE-3 not prominently surfaced in search results.
- ACS guidelines are produced through both the Trauma Quality Programs (TQP) Best Practices pathway and through Guideline Central partnerships; both types are listed.
- Several ACC/AHA guidelines are co-authored with ACEP, HRS, SCAI, and other societies — these are listed under the primary co-authoring pair.
- SCCM's Surviving Sepsis Campaign last published full international guidelines in 2021; no major full update found for 2023–2025 (focused updates on corticosteroids and bundles were issued).
- AAN's 2024 neurology guideline highlights include stroke, epilepsy, and movement disorders — individual titles available at https://www.neurologylive.com/view/blueprints-better-care-2024-neurology-guideline-highlights
