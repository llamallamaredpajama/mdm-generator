## Answers You Need, When You Need Them

Cough, fever, runny nose, fatigue. These symptoms can be assigned to any number of respiratory infections, making diagnosis a potentially dangerous guessing game. To improve patient outcomes and therapy management, your test should give you actionable answers as quickly as possible. Here’s how our multiplex molecular test addresses that challenge.

![](https://s7g10.scene7.com/is/image/biomerieux/CLIPBOARD?qlt=85&wid=%7B.width%7D&ts=1712349494320&dpr=on,1)

## **Identify Pathogens More Often**

The BIOFIRE RP2.1 Panel has a positivity rate of 87.2% compared to 30.2% in rapid antigen tests.2

![](https://s7g10.scene7.com/is/image/biomerieux/SPEED-3?qlt=85&wid=%7B.width%7D&ts=1710956550548&dpr=on,1)

## **Faster Time to Results**

The panel’s turnaround time for influenza and non-influenza viruses, respectively, is 1.7 hours and 1.5 hours. This compares to 7.7 hours and 13.5 hours, respectively, in standard of care.3

![](https://s7g10.scene7.com/is/image/biomerieux/CUSTOMER_SATISFACTION-3?qlt=85&wid=%7B.width%7D&ts=1710956563601&dpr=on,1)

## **Improve Antimicrobial Therapy**

The RP2.1 Panel has been shown to improve targeted broad-spectrum antimicrobial therapy and reduce antimicrobial duration in patients by as much as 24 hours.4

![](https://s7g10.scene7.com/is/image/biomerieux/DIAGNOSTICS-3?qlt=85&wid=%7B.width%7D&ts=1715092915404&dpr=on,1)
